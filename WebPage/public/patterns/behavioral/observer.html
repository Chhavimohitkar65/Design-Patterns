Observer Pattern Demo

A real-world implementation showing how social media notifications work

How it Works

The Observer pattern establishes a subscription mechanism where observers (subscribers) are notified when changes occur in the subject (publisher). In this demo:

  • The Post Creator is the subject (publisher)
  • Different types of notifications are observers (subscribers)
  • When a new post is created, all active subscribers are notified
